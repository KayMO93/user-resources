How to set daily goal in Waterlogged

Here's how you set a daily goal in Waterlogged.

  1. Tap the 'Settings' icon in the top-left corner.
  2. Tap 'Daily Goal.'
  3. Type in your desired amount of water and the proper unit (oz, mL, or L).
  4. Tap 'Save' to be automatically returned to the 'Settings' tab.
Your daily goal is now set.
