How to log your water intake in Waterlogged

Here's how you log your water intake in Waterlogged.

  1. Open the Waterlogged app. On the home screen, ensure that the date you are logging is selected at the top. When your date is selected, tap the plus sign at the center of the container.
  2. Select the type of container you're drinking from with the amount of liquid you're drinking. (If your choice of container is not displayed, tap 'More choices', scroll left, and tap 'add new container.' Select 'change' to add a new photo of your container. Rename it and adjust the amount of liquid in the container in either oz, mL, or L. Containers can also be added by going to 'Settings' and 'Containers.')
  3. Tap the arrow in the top left corner to go back to the 'More choices' screen. On this same screen, you can also select the type of liquid in your container.
  4. After imputing your information, save your selections to be automatically returned to the home screen.
  5. Repeat these steps to add more liquid intake to your log for the day.
Your water intake is now logged for the day.
